Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does GrowthMap work?

Simply create an account, take our 20-question assessment, and our AI will analyze your interests and skills to recommend the best ICT careers for you - complete with a personalized qualification roadmap.

Is this really free?

Yes! GrowthMap is completely free for all Sri Lankan students. We believe everyone deserves access to quality career guidance, regardless of their background.

How long does the assessment take?

Most students complete the assessment in just 5 minutes. It's quick, easy, and gives you instant results with your personalized career roadmap.

What qualifications do you support?

We cover all major Sri Lankan qualifications including NVQ Levels 4-7, Diplomas, and Degrees from recognized institutions across the country.

Can I change my career path later?

Absolutely! Your roadmap is a living document. As your interests evolve, you can retake the assessment and get updated recommendations anytime.

Do I need prior ICT experience?

Not at all! GrowthMap is designed for complete beginners. We explain everything in simple terms and help you discover paths you might not have known existed.
